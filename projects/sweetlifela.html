HBO Max SWEET LIFE: LOS ANGELES

Digital Concept Renders and Drafting for a HBO Max Reality TV Show. “The House”.

Project Summary: Sweet Life: Los Angeles is an American reality television series that follows a close group of African-American friends in their 20s, as they live their lives in South Los Angeles striving for "Black Excellence. For the project I created a number of pre-vis renders and drawings for the interview sets based on different design styles as conceptualized by the Art Director.

Art Direction & Design By: Samantha McCullough
Concept Renders & Set Design By: Dazzy Shah
