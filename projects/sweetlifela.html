SWEET LIFE: LOS ANGELES (HBO MAX)

Sweet Life: Los Angeles is a reality television series centered on a close group of African-American friends in their 20s, capturing their personal journeys, relationships, and ambitions in South Los Angeles through the lens of “Black Excellence.”

Across Episodes 1–5, I created a range of concept renders and pre-vis drawings for the confessional interview environments. Working from design directions established by the Art Director, these visuals explored varied spatial layouts, textures, and visual moods, serving as a tool to refine the aesthetic direction and communicate the intended atmosphere of each interview set prior to build.

Art Director – Samantha McCullough
Concept & Set Designer – Dazzy Shah
