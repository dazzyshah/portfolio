Disney+ "DRIVING HOME 2 U" – AFTER PARTY

Assistant Art Direction for Olivia Rodrigo's Disney+ Documentary– “Driving Home 2 U”–Premiere Event & After Party.

Project Summary: "Driving Home 2 U" is a documentary about Olivia Rodrigo's road trip between Salt Lake City & Los Angeles, while performing songs from her album Sour. As the Assistant Art Director for the after party, I created drawings, layouts, and 3D models crucial to the execution of the design.

Production Designer: Darcy Scanlin
Art Director: Charles Tyler Tylinski
