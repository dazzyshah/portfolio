MICKEY & FRIENDS HOLIDAY VILLAGE

Set Design & Reference Documentation for “Disney's Holiday Village" .

Project Summary: Disney’s Holiday Village was a two-day marketplace event held in Venice, CA to kickoff the Holiday Season with Disney-themed goods from various brands including Forever 21, Coach, and more. The original design for this event was sent over by the client, which was then re-worked upon for building and fabrication purposes.

Set Designers: Dazzy Shah & Alex Opferman
Fabrication & Installtion by: Cactus Fabrication
