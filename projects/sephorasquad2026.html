SEPHORA SQUAD 2026 LAUNCH EVENT (SEPHORA)

Sephora Squad 2026 Launch Event officially welcomed the newest class of creators into Sephora’s vibrant community. Our mission was to get the Squad hyped and ready to roll with a high-energy, social-first, three-day experience featuring a welcome dinner, a brunch, and studio sessions – all brought to life with diverse brand partners and speakers. Inspired by the worlds of print, press, and content creation, the event transformed creators into headline news, culminating in their feature debut as the stars of a bold, buzzworthy News Drop.

As Lead Environmental Designer on the project, I played a key role in shaping and curating every 3D touchpoint under Katy’s creative direction — from designing dynamic, immersive table layouts for the dinner and brunch events, to creating renders that clearly communicated our vision and intent. I also curated the finer details, including furniture and décor selections for table scapes and brand booths, ensuring every element felt cohesive, elevated, and on-theme.

Creative Agency – MKG
Creative Director – Katy Bohurjak
Lead Experiential Designer – Dazzy Shah

Duke's @ Sunset Tower

DAY 2 – STUDIO SESSIONS

Sephora Studios
Brand Partners – Phlur, Ouai, Dr Idriss & Armani

Creators were greeted by a scene straight out of Sephora Street upon arrival to the studios – a wall of wheat-pasted posters featured the newest class of Squad members, a flower-bombed bus shelter photo moment, and three streetfront inspired brand booths.

The brand booths were built in collaboration with our partner brands: Armani, Dr. Idriss, Ouai, and Phlur. Armani was inspired by a sleek high-end boutique, Dr. Idriss a modern pharmacy, Ouai a chic hotel front, and Phlur an inviting café.

Finally, speakers presented against a beautiful stylized draped stage backdrop.
