greetings!

My name is Dazzy Shah*, and I am an Indian-Canadian Production Designer, currently based in the Los Angeles area. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Architecture and Visual Studies Degree from the University of Toronto. The combination of my studies helped me explore various forms of artistic practices, which ultimately led me to finding my passion for Production Design! I firmly believe that just as Good Design can change the world that we live in, good Production Design can further add to the content of a story by giving its characters a stronger anchor to ground them in.

I am currently an M.F.A. in Production Design Candidate at the Dodge School of Film and Media Arts at Chapman University, California.

Apart from my dire love for Production Design, I have also explored other realms of spatial design including Interior Design, Architectural Design, Set Design for the Theatre and Event Design. When not buried in a stack of Pinterest boards and books, I enjoy writing, reading, floor naps and binging my ever-expanding list of media recommendations.

*Daisy Sha
